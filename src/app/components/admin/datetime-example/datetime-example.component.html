DateTime Examples

baw-datetime

Date & Time Instants: 
<baw-datetime [value]="fakeDate" />
Date Instants: 
<baw-datetime [value]="fakeDate" date />
Time Instants: 
<baw-datetime [value]="fakeDate" time />

baw-zoned-datetime (Implicit)

Zoned Date & Time Instants: 
<baw-zoned-datetime [value]="fakeDate" />
Zoned Date & Time Instants: 
<baw-zoned-datetime [value]="fakeDate" date />
Zoned Date & Time Instants: 
<baw-zoned-datetime [value]="fakeDate" time />

baw-zoned-datetime (Explicit)

Zoned Date & Times: 
<baw-zoned-datetime [value]="fakeDate" [timezone]="fakeSiteTimezone" />
Zoned Dates: 
<baw-zoned-datetime [value]="fakeDate" [timezone]="fakeSiteTimezone" date />
Zoned Times: 
<baw-zoned-datetime [value]="fakeDate" [timezone]="fakeSiteTimezone" time />

baw-duration

Durations (sexagesimal): 
<baw-duration [value]="fakeDuration" />
Duration (ISO 8601): 
<baw-duration [value]="fakeDuration" iso8601 />
Duration (Humanized): 
<baw-duration [value]="fakeDuration" humanized />

baw-time-since

Relative Time Instants: 
<baw-time-since [value]="fakeDuration" />
Relative Date Instants: 
<baw-time-since [value]="fakeDate" />
